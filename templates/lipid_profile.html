How it works:

Our system uses advanced text extraction and computer vision to analyze your lab report and identify your lipid values automatically. For best results:

  • Upload a clear, high-quality PDF
  • Ensure the report contains standard lipid panel results
  • The report should include Total Cholesterol, HDL, LDL, and Triglycerides values
Reference Ranges:

Total Cholesterol:
< 200 mg/dL: Desirable
200-239 mg/dL: Borderline high
≥ 240 mg/dL: High

HDL Cholesterol:
< 40 mg/dL: Low
40-59 mg/dL: Normal
≥ 60 mg/dL: Optimal

LDL Cholesterol:
< 100 mg/dL: Optimal
100-129 mg/dL: Near optimal
130-159 mg/dL: Borderline high
160-189 mg/dL: High
≥ 190 mg/dL: Very high

Triglycerides:
< 150 mg/dL: Normal
150-199 mg/dL: Borderline high
200-499 mg/dL: High
≥ 500 mg/dL: Very high
